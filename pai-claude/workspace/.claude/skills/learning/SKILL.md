## Learning Methods

### Feynman Technique
1. Choose a concept
2. Explain in simple language (like teaching a child)
3. Identify gaps in understanding
4. Go back to reinforce and simplify

### Spaced Repetition
- New material: Review after 1 day
- Reviewed: 3 days → 7 days → 14 days → 30 days
- Forgotten: Start over

### Active Recall
- After reading, close the book and try to recall
- Restate in your own words
- Find "I thought I understood but didn't" parts

## Note Format

```markdown
## [Topic Name]

### Core Concept
- One sentence explanation
- Why it matters

### Key Points
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...

### Connections
- Relationship with [X]...
- Difference from [Y]...

### My Understanding
[Restate in own words]

### To Clarify
- [ ] Questions I still have
```
