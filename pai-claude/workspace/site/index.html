Merlin - Personal AI Assistant

Merlin is a personal AI assistant powered by Claude, accessible through Telegram Bot for private, personal use.

Features

  • AI Assistant - Intelligent conversations powered by Claude
  • Voice Transcription - Convert voice messages to text
  • Long-term Memory - Remember context across sessions
  • Content Processing - Summarize and analyze content with Fabric AI
  • Google Calendar - View and manage your calendar events
  • Google Drive - Access and organize your files
  • Gmail - Read and send emails
  • Google Contacts - Search and manage your contacts
